About Me

Hi, I’m Dr.Eric Kantona

Services

  • Routine prenatal check-ups.
  • Ultrasound and fetal monitoring.
  • Nutritional and lifestyle guidance for pregnant women.
  • Monitoring and managing complications like gestational diabetes, preeclampsia, and preterm labor.
  • Specialized care for multiple pregnancies (twins, triplets, etc.).
  • Vaginal delivery.
  • Assisted delivery (vacuum extraction or forceps).
  • Cesarean section (C-section).
  • Postpartum check-ups.
  • Breastfeeding support and guidance.
  • Postpartum depression management.
  • Birth control counseling and prescriptions.
  • Intrauterine device (IUD) insertion and removal.
  • Contraceptive implant insertion and removal.
  • Pap smears for cervical cancer screening.
  • Pelvic exams.
  • Breast exams.
  • Treatment for heavy periods (menorrhagia).
  • Painful periods (dysmenorrhea) and irregular cycles.
  • Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS).
  • Endometriosis.
  • Uterine fibroids.
  • Fertility assessments.
  • Assisted reproductive technologies (ART) like IVF (In Vitro Fertilization).
  • Ovarian, uterine, cervical, and vulvar cancer screenings.
  • Colposcopy and biopsy for abnormal Pap smears.
  • Diagnosis and management of PID and other sexually transmitted infections (STIs).
  • Hormone replacement therapy (HRT).
  • Management of menopause-related symptoms like hot flashes and osteoporosis.
  • Surgical removal of the uterus (abdominal, vaginal, or laparoscopic hysterectomy).
  • Surgical removal of uterine fibroids while preserving the uterus.
  • Laparoscopic treatment of ovarian cysts.
  • Endometriosis resection.
  • Diagnostic laparoscopy for pelvic pain or infertility.
  • Egg freezing and embryo preservation for future pregnancies.
  • Ovarian tissue preservation.
  • Permanent sterilization procedure (tying or sealing fallopian tubes).
  • Procedure to remove tissue from the uterus, often after miscarriage or to treat heavy bleeding.
  • Treatment for pelvic organ prolapse.
  • Surgical repair of vaginal or uterine prolapse.
  • Stitching the cervix during pregnancy to prevent premature labor in high-risk pregnancies.
  • Procedure to remove or destroy the lining of the uterus to treat heavy menstrual bleeding.
  • Surgical removal of one or both ovaries.
  • Surgical or non-surgical procedures to tighten or restore the vagina.
